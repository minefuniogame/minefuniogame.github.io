About Krunker.io: A Timeless Classic

Krunker.io is a fast-paced browser FPS that delivers competitive shooting with fluid movement and instant action, all without downloads.

Since 2018, it has stood out with its class-based system, high skill ceiling, and strong community support.

How to Play: Understanding the Core Mechanics

Krunker.io rewards speed, accuracy, and smart class selection.

Classes: Different weapons and health pools support varied playstyles.

Movement: Slide-hopping is essential for survival.

Combat: Fast reactions and headshots win fights.

Game Modes and Objectives Explained in Detail

Krunker.io offers multiple modes to keep gameplay fresh and competitive:

  • FFA: Score the most kills individually.
  • TDM: Work with teammates to dominate the map.
  • Hardpoint: Capture and defend rotating zones.
  • CTF: Steal the enemy flag while defending yours.
  • Custom Modes: Parkour, Infection, and more.

Controls: Mastering the Intuitive Controls

Mastering the movement and hotkeys in Krunker.io is essential for competitive play. The game follows a standard PC shooter layout but adds specific keys for its unique physics.

For high-level players, the Space + Shift rhythm is the most important. Being able to chain these keys in less than a second is what allows you to maintain top speeds. Similarly, using the F key to spray can be used tactically to mark enemy territory or distract opponents in custom lobbies.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. What is the best class in Krunker.io?

There is no "best" class, but the Triggerman and Run N Gun are the most popular for beginners due to their versatility and ease of use.

2. How do I get free KR?

You earn KR by playing matches and scoring points. You can also get "Free KR" every few hours by clicking the button in the shop menu and watching a short ad.

3. Why is my game lagging?

Check your server region in the bottom right of the home screen. Ensure you are on the server closest to you. Also, try lowering your "Resolution Scale" in the settings.

4. How do I slide hop?

Jump (Space), and as you are falling, hold Crouch (Shift). When you hit the ground, jump again immediately. Repeat this in a rhythm to build speed.

5. What are "Mods" and how do I use them?

Mods are community-made packs that change the game's visuals and sounds. You can find them in the "Mods" tab on the main menu and load them with a single click.

6. Can I play Krunker with a controller?

Yes, the game has basic controller support, though it is not recommended for competitive play as mouse and keyboard are much more precise.

7. How do I join a clan?

Go to the Krunker Hub and look at the "Clans" tab. You can apply to join clans, but many require you to reach a certain level (usually level 10 or 20) first.

8. How do I trade skins?

You must be level 20 to trade. Once you reach level 20, you can use the Trade servers or send trade offers directly through the Krunker Hub.

9. What is a "Nuke" and how do I get one?

A Nuke is a killstreak reward. You must get 25 kills without dying in a single match. Once achieved, you can press "1" to call it in and kill every enemy on the map.

10. Is Krunker.io dying?

Absolutely not. The game maintains a massive player base and receives weekly updates from the developers, including new maps, skins, and balance changes.
